THE HUDDLE: PALM SPRINGS

We had yet another incredible Huddle last week, this time in Palm Springs with a fully vaccinated cohort! Here are a few photos from the week and details on the event…

It’s been over a year since we’ve had a Huddle! This Palm Springs Huddle (our 5th Huddle) was originally scheduled for June of 2020 and obviously the pandemic created some major hurdles in making it happen. And when it finally did last week, there was so much pent up energy and excitement in this incredible group of female founders growing and scaling big, impressive dreams and businesses. PS Our October Huddle in Austin, Texas was just announced a couple weeks ago and spots are going fast as we accept on a rolling basis, so if you’re interested don’t wait to apply!

This time we headed to Palm Springs to a gorgeous estate and incredible pool. Our retreats are a real mix between deep dives into business strategy, individualized support for your business, holistic care, wellness, family style meals, and time for connection. We work hard and play hard!
